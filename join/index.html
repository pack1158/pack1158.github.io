Join Cub Scouts

We will be holding a Join Scouting Night recruitment event this fall, typically around the third Wednesday of September, in the Ashburn Elementary school cafeteria. Continue to check back here for up-to-date information.

Please note that Pack 1158 has made the decision to remain an all boy pack for the upcoming scout year. If you are specifically looking for a co-ed pack participating in the Family Scouting program, we would be happy to refer you to any of the wonderful area packs who are offering co-ed programs.

In the meantime, take a look at the following Pack 1158 resources.

  • Handbook
  • Calendar

You can fill out an inquiry form and we will be sure to reach back out to you about membership in Pack 1158.

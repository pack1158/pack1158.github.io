Join Scouting Night

  • Date: Tuesday, 11 September 2018
  • Time: 7:00 pm
  • Location: Ashburn Elementary Cafeteria

Please come and join us on Tuesday, September 11 to learn more about Pack 1158! We will discuss the basics of cub scouting, our pack structure, annual events, and dues. This event is primarily for parents of prospective scouts, but children are welcome and will have an opportunity to play games with older scouts.
